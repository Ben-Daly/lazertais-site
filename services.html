Our Studio

Our studio has state of the art equipment for all of your recording needs. We have the latest Apple DAW software (Logic Pro X) which works in conjunction with our 24 input audio interface. We also have a whole inventory of cymbals, snare drums, guitar & bass amplification and much more for you to use when you come into the studio. Microphones are an importance in our studio, we have the best studio microphones that money can buy which really show on recordings that have been recorded here at Lazertai's.

The Workshop

Our workshop is the home of guitar modifications. If it's a re-string or a re-fret, we'll carryout a quality job on your instrument ensuring that the original material fidelity is kept intact. Our prices for the services we offer are stated below:


Service Price Description
Re-string & setup £15 We will re-string your guitar with your choice of string, if you're unsure on what your string preference is, we will suggest one for you based on the type of music you play.
Pickup change £30 + bridge cost We will change the pickups in your guitar to your specification, if we believe that your chosen pickup is incompatible for your guitar, we will notify you
Bridge changee £30 per pickup + pickup price We will change the bridge on your guitar to your specification, as long as it's compatible
Scalloped fretboard £30 per hour We can scallop your fretboard to your desired depth.
Re-fret £60 + cost of new frets We can re-fret your guitar if the frets are old & tired out.
